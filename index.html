Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our laundry service.

How does pickup and delivery work?

We offer free pickup and delivery within our service area. Simply schedule a pickup time through our website or app, and we'll come to your home or office to collect your laundry. After cleaning, we'll return it to you at your preferred delivery time, folded and ready to wear.

What are your operating hours?

We offer pickup and delivery services 7 days a week from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Our cleaning facility operates 24/7 to ensure quick turnaround times. Customer support is available 24/7 via phone, email, or in-app chat.

Do I need to be home for pickup/delivery?

No, you don't need to be home if you provide us with special instructions. Many customers leave their laundry in a secure location (porch, lobby, etc.) for pickup and specify a delivery location. We'll text you when we've completed both pickup and delivery.

What safety measures do you have in place?

We take hygiene seriously. All laundry is washed at high temperatures with hospital-grade detergents. Our staff wears masks and gloves, and we sanitize all bags and equipment between uses. We also offer contactless pickup and delivery options.

How do I pay for the service?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Venmo. Payment is processed securely through our website or app after your laundry is weighed and before delivery. You'll receive an itemized receipt with your clean laundry.
